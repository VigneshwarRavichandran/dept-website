About seminars

Seminars are public presentations of research or other work given by graduate students, faculty members, and other professionals. The purpose of seminars is to give students the experience of researching the scientific literature, organizing data, assembling and delivering a presentation, and discussing scientific research.In IT Department,we frequently conduct many seminars for students to enhance their technical knowledge.
