PLACEMENTS

Department of Training and Placement plays a vital role in making students industry ready. Our primary objective is to prepare students for the real world. We organize a lot of campus recruitment drives for both placements and internships. We constantly collaborate with industries for various skill development programs.The Department Of Information Technology ensures every student get placed.

ACTIVITIES

All final year students are exposed / trained in the following areas:

  • Career Awareness sessions
  • Model Aptitude tests, Mock Group discussions & Interviews to excel in the Recruitment process
  • Guidance sessions for higher studies
  • Arranging In-plant training, Industrial visits, Projects, Guest lectures and other Industry-Institute interaction activities

All leading recruiters visit us to hire productive professional from our department. Following are some of our recruiters.

TIPS TO OUTSHINE OTHERS AT CAMPUS PLACEMENTS

  • PLACEMENTS
  • RESUME WRITING

Start by preparing specifically for the companies you are sitting for.

Pick the language of your choice and then learn to code all sort of programs using it.

Make a crisp and effective resume.Your resume is a window to present yourself briefly.

Data Structures, Algorithms, Operating Systems are the subjects most interviewers focus on.

Go through the Recruitment Processes and Test Patterns of companies visiting our campus.

Take practice runs for all the skill assessments, by accessing different mock tests for all the areas.

Your resume descriptions will require precise language that should sound professional and must be attractive.

Highlight the most relevant experiences and skills and Make your experiences sound impressive and responsible


Include information about the successes you achieved in a certain role also Appreciate yourself as an engaged learner

Choose the Best Resume Format and Make sure it is formatted and aligned properly in such a way that it is easy to read.
